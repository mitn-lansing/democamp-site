What is Demo Camp?

Demo Camp Lansing is where our awesome tech community comes together to showcase what they've built. From established companies demonstrating their latest innovations to startups unveiling game-changing products, from individual creators sharing passion projects to teams presenting enterprise solutions - this is your stage to share with fellow innovators.

In a relaxed, friendly atmosphere, you'll experience the full breadth of Lansing's tech ecosystem. See cutting-edge technology from MSU researchers and local companies to garage tinkerers, meet passionate creators from all backgrounds, and discover what's emerging in our thriving, diverse tech scene. It's part show-and-tell, part networking, and all about celebrating the incredible things our community is building.
